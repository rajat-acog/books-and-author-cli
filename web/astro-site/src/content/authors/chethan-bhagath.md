## one night at the call centre

**Year:** 2005

**Summary:** "One Night at the Call Centre" by Chetan Bhagat is a story about a group of friends who work at a call center in India. The book is told from the perspective of one of the main characters, Shyam, who is having a really bad day.

On this particular night, Shyam and his friends are all dealing with their own personal problems: relationships gone wrong, career doubts, and financial worries. Just when things seem to be at their worst, they all receive a mysterious phone call. This call claims to be from God, and it gives them each specific instructions and predictions about their lives.

As the night unfolds, the friends are forced to confront their fears, make difficult choices, and re-evaluate their lives based on the cryptic messages they receive. The book explores themes of friendship, love, career struggles, and the search for meaning in life, all wrapped up in a fast-paced and relatable narrative. It's a story about a single, transformative night that changes the perspective of these ordinary young people.
