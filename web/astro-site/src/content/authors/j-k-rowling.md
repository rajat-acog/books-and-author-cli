## Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

**Year:** 1997

**Summary:** Harry Potter is an ordinary boy living with his unkind aunt and uncle. He doesn't know he's special until letters start arriving for him. The letters are invitations to a magic school called Hogwarts.

At Hogwarts, Harry learns he's a wizard and makes friends, Ron and Hermione. He discovers that his parents were powerful wizards killed by an evil wizard named Voldemort when Harry was just a baby.

Harry and his friends learn about the Philosopher's Stone, a magical object that can make you immortal. They suspect someone is trying to steal it from the school. Through thrilling adventures, they uncover a plot to bring Voldemort back to power and must race against time to protect the stone and stop him.

**Description:** Turning the envelope over, his hand trembling, Harry saw a purple wax seal bearing a coat of arms; a lion, an eagle, a badger and a snake surrounding a large letter 'H'.

HARRY POTTER has never even heard of Hogwarts when the LETTERS start dropping on the doormat at number four, Privet Drive.

## Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

**Year:** 1998

**Summary:** In "Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets," Harry Potter is trying to enjoy his summer vacation after his first year at Hogwarts. However, a house-elf named Dobby keeps appearing to warn him about danger. When Harry returns to school for his second year, he starts hearing strange whispers that nobody else seems to notice.

**Description:** Throughout the summer holidays after his first year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, Harry Potter has been receiving sinister warnings from a house-elf called Dobby.

Now, back at school to start his second year, Harry hears unintelligible whispers echoing through the corridors.
